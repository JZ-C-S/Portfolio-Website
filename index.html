About Me

Ever since I was a kid, I have loved playing games and always wondered how they were made and brought to life. That curiosity slowly turned into a real interest in coding, and over time, I found myself exploring concepts like game development and website design. That’s what led me to study Computer Science and start creating projects of my own.

I’ve gained experience with several programming languages and tools, including Python, Java, C++, C, C# (using Unity), Haskell, and web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Recently, I’ve been focusing on Unity projects, building small game replicas to learn more about design, logic, and performance — and honestly, just to have fun with it.

What keeps me motivated is seeing how code can turn ideas into something real. Whether it’s a game, an app, or a website, I love the challenge of building things that work and look good. I’m always looking for new opportunities to learn, experiment, and keep growing as a developer.
